MY BACKGROUND

My life story is one of resilience and determination. At 27, I've journeyed through various phases that have shaped who i am today. I began your education at Saniak Primary School, laying the foundational knowledge that would support my future aspirations. After primary school, I progressed to Kapsabet Girls High School, where I likely honed not only my academic skills but also my leadership and social abilities. My passion for education led me to Kenyatta University, where I pursued a Bachelor of Education with a focus on Biology and Agriculture. University life presented its challenges, but i demonstrated incredible resourcefulness by becoming a grocery vendor. ways of Enganging young people

  1. Community Clean-Up Days:Organize events where young people can come together to clean up parks, streets, or other public spaces, promoting environmental stewardship.
  2. Food Drives:Coordinate food collection campaigns for local food banks or shelters, encouraging youth to donate non-perishable items or volunteer to sort and pack food.
  3. Tutoring Programs:Establish tutoring or mentoring sessions for younger students, allowing young people to share their knowledge and skills while fostering community ties.
  4. Youth Leadership Workshops:Host workshops focusing on leadership skills, empowering young people to take initiative and lead community projects.
  5. Sports and Recreation Clinics:Organize sports activities or clinics for younger children, promoting teamwork, physical fitness, and community bonding.
  6. Art and Cultural Projects:Encourage creative expression through murals, community art projects, or performances that enhance local culture and beauty.
  7. Environmental InitiativesStart gardening clubs or environmental awareness campaigns, teaching young people about sustainability while beautifying the community.
  8. Senior Citizen Outreach:Facilitate visits to senior centers where young people can engage with elderly residents through games, storytelling, or tech help.
  9. Health Awareness Campaigns:Organize health fairs or workshops focusing on important topics such as nutrition, fitness, and mental health, engaging youth in promoting well-being.
  10. Fundraising Events:Plan community fundraising events for local charities or causes, involving young people in organizing and participating to support their community.
  11. Animal Shelter Volunteering:Coordinate visits to local animal shelters, allowing young people to help care for animals and promote animal welfare.
  12. Cultural Exchange Programs:Create opportunities for youth to learn about different cultures through exchange programs, fostering understanding and inclusivity.
  13. Skill-Building Workshops:Organize workshops where young people can learn useful skills (e.g., cooking, first aid) and then share those skills with others in the community.
  14. Literacy Programs:Start reading programs for children, where young people can help promote literacy and a love for reading.
  15. Advocacy Campaigns:Encourage young people to participate in advocacy for local issues, helping them understand civic engagement and the importance of their voices.
